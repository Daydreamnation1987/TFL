*Fuzz Not Bombs*

Low, Growly, Sludgy, Full-Bodied: there are so many descriptions that fit this pedal!

The Fuzz Not Bombs, Moonman's take on a modern classic, has dedicated FUZZ, TONE, GATE, and VOLUME controls that all interact beautifully with each other to create some amazing, saturated fuzz sounds. Everything from Doomy Sludge to Dirty Synth-Pop, this is a great full-range pedal equally at home with Bass, Guitar, Keys, or even Vocals!

Based around the Zvex Wooly Mammoth circuit, the Fuzz Not Bombs has a modified input Hi-pass filter and TONE circuit to allow for more hi-mid content on the output. This pedal really shines on bass, and sounds glorious with both single-coil and humbucker outfitted guitars.
